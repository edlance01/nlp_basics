In a **Bag-of-Words (BoW) or TF-IDF** vector space, words are positioned based purely on their frequency or co-occurrence, **not** their meaning.
As a result, semantically similar words (**King** and **Queen**) are often treated as equally distant from each other as they are from an unrelated word (**Banana**).
